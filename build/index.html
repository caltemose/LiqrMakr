Making Your Own Liquors is Easy

LiqrMakr helps you make your own liquors at home using grain alcohol and a variety of flavoring agents. We have recipes for all sorts of liquors including limoncello, coffee liquor, anisette and even home-made gin.

How You Do It

This section shows an illustrated step-by-step of the process.

  1. Mix

    Mix grain alcohol (95% alcohol) with flavoring ingredient(s)

  2. Wait

    Anywhere from a few days to a month

  3. Finish

    1. Filter the mixture
    2. Add water or simple syrup
    3. Bottle
