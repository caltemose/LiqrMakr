Cinnamoncello

Hot, spicy, woody goodness.

This recipe will make ml of cinnamoncello at percent (76 proof) using grain alcohol at percent.

Yield: xml container of liquor.

1. Mix

Mix these ingredients and store in an airtight glass container.

  • ml grain alcohol (at 95%).
  • LBS of cinnamon sticks

2. Wait

30 days. A few days more is fine. Make the simple syrup the day before the flavored alcohol is ready to be bottled. You'll need:

  • 250ml water
  • 120ml sugar

Heat the water, add the sugar and dissolve and let the syrup cool before adding to macerated alcohol.

3. Finish

  • Filter the macerated alcohol. If you just remove the sticks, you'll have a cloudy and very woody cinnamoncello. Filter through a coffee filter to clear up the liquor and remove all the cinnamon stick residue.
  • Add the simple syrup to the macerated alcohol. Do this in a container that will make it easy to pour the result using a funnel into your final bottle.
  • Bottle the results.
