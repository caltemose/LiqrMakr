Star Anise Liquor

What to say about this recipe...

This recipe will make ml of liquor at percent (76 proof) using grain alcohol at percent.

Yield: xml container of liquor.

1. Mix

Mix these ingredients and store in an airtight glass container.

  • ml grain alcohol (at 95%).
  • grams of star anise.
  • grams of coriander seeds.
  • cinnamon sticks.
  • cloves
  • vanilla pod(s).
  • the peel of orange(s).

2. Wait

20 days minimum. You'll need:

  • 250ml water
  • 120ml sugar

Heat the water, add the sugar and dissolve and let the syrup cool before adding to macerated alcohol.

3. Finish

  • Filter the macerated alcohol.
  • Add the simple syrup to the macerated alcohol. Do this in a container that will make it easy to pour the result using a funnel into your final bottle.
  • Bottle the results. Drink immediately or let it sit for a while.
